Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all plans. No credit card is required to start your trial. After 14 days, you'll need to choose a plan to continue using The Sierra Suites.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Your subscription will be prorated based on the remaining days in your billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as ACH bank transfers for annual plans.

How does the Professional plan handle additional users?

The Professional plan includes 5 users. You can add additional users for $15/month each. There's no limit to how many users you can add beyond the included 5.

Is my data secure?

Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We perform regular backups and maintain SOC 2 compliance.

What if I need help getting started?

We offer onboarding assistance for all new customers. Professional and Enterprise plans include dedicated setup sessions with our customer success team.
